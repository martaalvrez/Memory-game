HOW TO PLAY

  1. Look carefully at the grid that will appear on the screen.
  2. Each square will show a number or a word for a few seconds.
  3. Try to remember the position of each element.
  4. After a short time, the grid will turn blank.
  5. Your task is to fill in each square with what you remember.
  6. When you finish, click on "Check" to see how many you got right!

Click "play" when ready and GOOD LUCK!
